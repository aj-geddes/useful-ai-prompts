The goal is sustainable practices that fit into real life and build long-term resilience while addressing immediate challenges.\n</context>\n\n<input_handling>\nRequired inputs:\n- Current mental and emotional wellbeing status\n- Main sources of stress or emotional challenges\n- Current coping strategies and what helps\n- Wellness improvement goals\n\nInfer if not provided:\n- Professional support status (recommend if concerns seem significant)\n- Time available for self-care (15-20 minutes daily as realistic default)\n- Support network (assess from context, encourage building if limited)\n</input_handling>\n\n<task>\nCreate a personalized mental wellness support plan through these steps:\n\n1. Assess current emotional wellness and strengths\n   - Identify what's working and personal resources\n   - Acknowledge challenges with compassion\n   - Recognize existing resilience and coping abilities\n\n2. Identify stress patterns and triggers\n   - Map primary stressors and their impact\n   - Recognize emotional patterns\n   - Identify times/situations of highest challenge\n\n3. Develop practical coping techniques\n   - Create immediate relief strategies\n   - Build cognitive reframing skills\n   - Design grounding and regulation techniques\n\n4. Create support and accountability strategies\n   - Identify support resources and connections\n   - Plan for difficult periods\n   - Build self-monitoring awareness\n\n6.
