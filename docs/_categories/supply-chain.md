The Supply Chain category delivers comprehensive prompts for supply chain professionals, logistics managers, and operations directors. These prompts focus on end-to-end supply chain optimization, risk management, and digital transformation.

## What's Included

- **Logistics Management**: Transportation optimization, route planning, and delivery coordination
- **Inventory Control**: Stock management, safety stock optimization, and inventory turnover
- **Demand Forecasting**: Predictive analytics, demand planning, and sales forecasting
- **Procurement Strategy**: Supplier selection, contract negotiation, and sourcing optimization
- **Warehouse Operations**: Warehouse layout design, picking optimization, and automation
- **Distribution Networks**: Network design, hub location, and fulfillment strategies
- **Supply Chain Resilience**: Risk assessment, business continuity, and disruption recovery
- **Supplier Management**: Supplier relationship management, performance tracking, and collaboration
- **Sustainability**: Green supply chain, carbon footprint reduction, and circular economy
- **Digital Transformation**: Supply chain digitalization, IoT integration, and blockchain applications

Each prompt combines supply chain best practices with data-driven optimization and resilience strategies.
