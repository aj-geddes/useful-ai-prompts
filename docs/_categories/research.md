The Research & Analysis category provides AI prompts for market researchers, UX researchers, competitive intelligence analysts, policy researchers, and business analysts. These prompts apply rigorous research methodology — sampling design, analysis frameworks, bias mitigation — to produce credible, actionable research outputs.

## What's Included

- **Primary Research Designer**: Research methodology selection, survey design, sampling strategy, and interview guide development
- **Competitive Intelligence Analyst**: Competitor analysis frameworks, market positioning maps, and intelligence synthesis for strategic decisions
- **Data Synthesis Expert**: Cross-source synthesis frameworks, finding triangulation, and insight development from complex datasets
- **Hypothesis Testing Advisor**: Experimental design, statistical approach selection, and results interpretation guidance
- **User Research Analyst**: Usability study design, qualitative coding frameworks, and user insight reporting
- **Secondary Research Specialist**: Literature review methodology, source evaluation criteria, and knowledge synthesis frameworks
- **Trend Analysis Expert**: Weak signal identification, trend validation frameworks, and strategic implication analysis
- **Research Report Writer**: Research report structure, executive summary writing, and finding visualization guidance

## Who Uses These Prompts

Researchers, analysts, and strategists who need AI to support the research process with methodological rigor — from study design through final deliverable — rather than just summarizing existing information.
