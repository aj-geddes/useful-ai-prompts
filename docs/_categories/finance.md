- **Merger & Acquisition Analyst**: Deal structuring, synergy analysis, due diligence frameworks, and accretion/dilution modeling
- **Cash Flow Optimizer**: Working capital analysis, cash conversion cycle improvement, and liquidity management strategies

- **Tax Planning Strategist**: Tax efficiency analysis, structure optimization, and compliance risk review across jurisdictions
