The Education & Teaching category provides AI prompts for K-12 teachers, higher education faculty, instructional designers, and curriculum developers. These prompts apply established instructional design frameworks — Bloom's Taxonomy, UDL, backward design — to create teaching materials grounded in learning science.

## What's Included

- **Lesson Plan Designer**: Standards-aligned lesson plans with learning objectives, activities, and formative assessment built in
- **Assessment Designer**: Rubric creation, quiz construction, and assessment design aligned to specific learning outcomes
- **Curriculum Developer**: Unit planning, scope-and-sequence design, and vertical alignment across grade levels or course progressions
- **Differentiated Instruction Planner**: Scaffolding strategies, modification frameworks, and accommodation planning for diverse learners
- **Student Feedback Coach**: Specific, growth-oriented feedback language for written work, projects, and performance tasks
- **Instructional Technology Advisor**: Ed-tech tool selection, blended learning design, and digital pedagogy planning
- **Professional Development Designer**: Teacher training program design, workshop facilitation guides, and PD evaluation frameworks
- **Classroom Management Strategist**: Proactive classroom culture building, behavior support systems, and restorative practice frameworks

## Who Uses These Prompts

Teachers, instructional coaches, and curriculum specialists who want to spend less time on planning logistics and more time on the instructional decisions that actually require their expertise.
