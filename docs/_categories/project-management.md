The Project Management category contains AI prompts for project managers, program managers, scrum masters, and project leads across industries. These prompts apply recognized PM frameworks — PMI, Agile, PRINCE2 — to the real constraints and stakeholder dynamics of your projects.

## What's Included

- **Project Charter Creator**: Structured project initiation documents covering scope, objectives, stakeholders, budget, and success criteria
- **Risk Register Manager**: Risk identification, probability-impact assessment, and mitigation planning tailored to your project type
- **Agile Sprint Planner**: Sprint goal definition, backlog refinement frameworks, velocity planning, and sprint retrospective facilitation
- **Stakeholder Communication Planner**: Communication matrix design, escalation paths, and status reporting templates
- **Milestone Planning Advisor**: Work breakdown structure development, dependency mapping, and schedule optimization
- **Resource Allocation Optimizer**: Capacity planning, skill-to-task matching, and resource conflict resolution frameworks
- **Scope Change Analyzer**: Change request evaluation, impact assessment, and change control documentation
- **Project Retrospective Facilitator**: Structured retrospective formats, lessons-learned documentation, and improvement action planning

## Who Uses These Prompts

Project managers and team leads who need to move faster on project documentation, stakeholder communications, and planning artifacts without sacrificing quality or rigor.
