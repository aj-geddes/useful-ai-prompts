The Healthcare Digital category provides specialized prompts for healthcare technology professionals, digital health innovators, and medical AI developers. These prompts address digital transformation, AI implementation, telehealth platforms, and patient-centered care solutions.

## What's Included

- **AI-Powered Diagnostics**: Medical imaging analysis, predictive diagnostics, and clinical decision support
- **Telehealth Platforms**: Virtual care infrastructure, remote patient monitoring, and digital consultations
- **Patient Data Analytics**: Healthcare data analysis, population health management, and outcomes tracking
- **EHR Integration**: Electronic health record systems, interoperability, and data exchange
- **Medical Device AI**: AI-enabled medical devices and wearable health technology
- **Clinical Workflows**: Digital workflow optimization and care coordination
- **Healthcare IoT**: Connected medical devices and real-time patient monitoring
- **Regulatory Compliance**: HIPAA, FDA, and healthcare data privacy frameworks
- **Patient Experience**: Digital patient engagement and personalized care solutions
- **Healthcare Innovation**: Digital health strategy and transformation roadmaps

Each prompt combines healthcare domain expertise with technology implementation strategies and regulatory compliance requirements.
